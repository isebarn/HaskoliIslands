PMBoK
Project Management Body of Knowledge. A guide that has all the accumulated knowledge
	that is generally recognized as a good practice

Product Backlog
A prioritized list of project requirements with estimated time to turn them into 
	complete product functionality. I.e a log that defines each feature and its cost

Fossalíkan/Waterfall
In the waterfall model the project is planned by defining all requirements up front, abd then
	a design is initiated, a build, integration, testing and deployment

Tæknivídd
Tæknivídd er einn hæfnisþátta verkefnastjóra, sá þáttur sem að snýr að því að byrja verkefni,
	stýra því og verklokum
	Tæknivíddin telur upp vinnu í kringum hagsmunaaðilla, áhættu og tækifæri, gæði,
	śtjórnskipulag, hópvinnu, úrlausn vandamála, greiningu verka, umfang, kostnað, tíma, 
	aðföng, breytingar, samninga, upplýsingar, samskipti, ræs og lok verkefnis

Samskiptaáætlun/Communication plan
A plan that defines information to be collected and distributed to stakeholders based on 
	their requirements
